Privacy Policy

Smart QR Hub
Developer: Casemull
Last updated: June 2026

Overview

Smart QR Hub is a mobile app published by Casemull. This policy describes how the app handles information on your device. It applies only to Smart QR Hub, not to other apps published by Casemull.

Information the App Accesses

Camera: The app uses your device camera only to scan QR codes on your device. Camera frames are processed in real time and are not stored, uploaded, or shared.

Photos: If you choose “Scan from photos,” the app reads the image you select to detect QR codes on-device. Images are not uploaded.

Information Stored on Your Device

Scan history: When you scan a code, the app may save the decoded content locally so you can view it later in History. This data stays on your device and is not sent to Casemull or third parties.

What We Do Not Do

  • We do not require an account or login
  • We do not collect personal information on our servers
  • We do not sell or share your data with advertisers
  • We do not use analytics or tracking SDKs in Smart QR Hub
  • We do not show advertisements in Smart QR Hub

Data Deletion

You can delete scan history at any time from Settings → Clear history. Deletion is permanent on your device.

Children

Smart QR Hub is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
