SiteQuote Pro

Last updated: June 2026

Overview

SiteQuote Pro helps specialty contractors create manual proposal drafts on site. In v1, proposal data stays on your device unless you choose to copy or share it outside the app.

Information the app accesses

Local storage: Proposal drafts, starter rate data, and settings are stored locally on your device.

Network links: Legal and support links may open your browser or email app when you tap them.

Future capabilities: Camera, microphone, location, PDF export, and paid Pro features are planned for a later version and are not part of the v1 submission.

Information stored on your device

Proposals, rate tables, and settings are stored locally. We do not upload your proposals to Casemull servers in the current version.

What we do not do

  • We do not require an account
  • We do not sell personal information
  • We do not use third-party advertising SDKs
